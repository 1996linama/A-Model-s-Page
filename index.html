About Her

Around 2014, Lien Phung began her career as a Chinese-American model while working part-time as a waitress. She was one of the few Asian-American to be featured on APSA*APSA and GlamSD. In 2017, Lien Phung organized a fashion show "The Lights on Us" in San Diego that was dedicated to showcasing Asian-American cultural roots and has even walked her very own runways. Since then, Lien has been gracing Instagram with her presence. She hopes to inspire others like herself to have confidence and pride in their own unique beauty. Her mission is to increase Asian-American representation in the modeling industry.

"If it ain't broke, keep workin'."
